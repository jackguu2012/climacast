ClimaCast was founded to inform.

Our goal is to provide accurate and timely weather forecasts, ensuring that you are always prepared for whatever the weather may bring. We believe that access to reliable weather information is essential for planning your day and keeping you and your loved ones safe.

Our Services

At ClimaCast, we offer services to help you stay informed about the weather. Our services include:

  • Real-time Weather: Get live updates on current weather conditions.
  • Daily Forecasts: Receive accurate daily weather forecasts tailored to your location.
  • Severe Weather Alerts: Stay safe with timely alerts for severe weather events.

Our Features

Explore our range of safety and weather features:

  • User-Friendly Interface: Navigate our site with ease and find the information you need quickly.
  • Mobile Compatibility: Access weather information on-the-go with our mobile-friendly site.
  • Interactive Maps: Explore weather patterns and forecasts with our interactive mapping tools.

Special Thanks

The services we use everyday to give you accurate updates:

  • Clime Weather & Radar: Clime is an intuitive Radar mapping application.
  • Met Office: The Met Office provide some of our Severe Weather Alerts.
  • OpenWeatherMap: OpenWeatherMap provides free weather data.
  • iFastNet: Website hosting provider.
